The 6 Automations Every Clinic Runs

Your front desk, running while you see patients.

Six admin jobs your reception does by hand today. The system does them when triggered: nothing clinical, nothing left to memory.

1
Appointment confirmation
Sent when a patient books: date, time, doctor, and clinic location in one message. No "did it go through?" calls.
2
Appointment reminder
Two touches: 24 hours before, and again 2 hours before the slot. Built to reduce empty chairs.
3
Bill & GST receipt
A GST invoice PDF on the WhatsApp thread when payment confirms. No printing, no "please send the bill again."
4
Report delivery
When your team signs off and uploads a pathology, ECG, X-ray, or USG report, the approved PDF is delivered to the patient with a verification QR. The system delivers the file. It does not read the report.
5
Follow-up reminder
"Your follow-up with Dr. [name] is due on [date]." Sent 7 days ahead, so the second visit is not left to memory.
6
Feedback & reviews
After the visit, every patient is asked for feedback and invited to leave a Google review. A low score also pings your team privately, so you can put it right before it becomes public.

Who Monitors It

Automation where it helps. Humans where it matters.

The system is built with clear rules, so your reception team knows what it handles and what comes back to them.

Working hours
Admin replies can run outside working hours. Anything needing a person is queued and flagged for your reception.
Handoff rules
Clinical questions, complaints, and anything the flow does not recognise route to your team with a clear patient message.
Failed replies
If the flow cannot answer twice, it stops trying and hands over. It does not loop.
Multi-branch support
Each branch can have its own number, timings, and queue, with one view across the group.

Does this chatbot diagnose patients?+
No. Logistics only: timings, bookings, reminders, reviews, and handoff. Any clinical question routes to your team.
What patient data does it hold?+
Name, phone number, preferred slot, and admin notes you approve. It does not see or store clinical records.

The flow can be configured for Hindi and English. We confirm the exact language behaviour during setup before anything goes live.
